# 2 Den

| Supply | Build                     |
| ------ | ------------------------- |
| 8      | House                     |
| 9      | x2 den > worker           |
| 10     | x3 wolf > house           |
| 16     | house >   2 wolf          |
| 20/20  | Worker > 2 wolf           |
| 30/30  | Worker rally to expansion |


OR


| Supply | Build                          |
| ------ | ------------------------------ |
| 9      | House                          |
| 10     | x2 den > worker                |
| 11     | wolf > house > continue wolves |
| 25     | third house > x2 wolf          |
| 29     | worker rally castle to nat     |


---

**10/10 build only (third house version):**

**if pressure is going well** delay castle and keep making wolves
**otherwise** make castle around 30-34 then resume workers then wolves.

---

**For either 2 den** its generally best to rally the 2nd & 3rd wolf around a sneaky path so ideally what happens is first wolf and next two wolves can meet up and push raider / worker off castle to delay it a little while on our side we use next 3 wolves to chase away their scouting raider and try to micro best you can to maximize dps on their isolated raiders.

---

**Cheese variations:**

**9/9:** **if you want more ideas** for different ways to commit atrocities soon as you hit supply cap ( got money for last wolves)  then you can pull all workers & try to keep wolves alive and let them regen while workers and last 2 wolves come across map

**10/10:** **with 10/10 build** after making 3rd house send across 2 workers to make towers or 1 tower + workshop as you have money.

---

**if opponent doesn't flub micro** they should be able to hold all of this just fine but its not easy if you aren't confident with micro. Kind of the nature of cheese I guess :P
