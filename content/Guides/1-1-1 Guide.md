## Early game 

 
 We will use raiders to be annoying on the map and do back stabs eventually along with gyro's though we do want to use our first gyro to scout primarily over harass especially if our raiders haven't confirmed a third 

once we see a third we want to go take our third. ideally we skip using our workshop all together early on and just take our third off of raider gyro with a smattering of soldiers or archers. 

If Opponents are staying 2 base longer than usual, and we are starting to bank gold? I generally find it best to also stay 2 base longer we have good tech prospects but poor straight forward fighting options early on. If they play a slow third and I go the cata route I  like to start making cuts to the rax here and there to afford other things faster.

- What does staying on 2 base longer mean? Making 2 cata's to be safe + adding on an advanced workshop + tower then eventually if they still don't take a third a forge then more cata. Gold that we'd be slowly accumulating for the 3rd castle nicely converts into cata(1) + aw then tower.

- Oh jeez but haffey I thought we hate towers you said they are horrible to make early? Typically yes I would emphatically nod here but we are investing so much into very technical units that are great backstabbers but poor fighters + small cata numbers on their own have very inconsistent damage and are very vulnerable early. The tower serves as an anchor / backbone for our defense at home since we are actually hoping for a base trade type scenario. then once our airship is ready the tower makes it easier to leave with our catas. (if the bulk of their units are near by your natural try to chip away at them with cata and airship micro safely) otherwise it can go aid in our harassment.

- Why AW we already have a Mill? Well If they are going to force us to make catapults earlier than is desirable, we don't want those catapults spending who knows how long as paper weights if our opponent doesn't actually attack. I prefer to flip the defensive cata into an offensive option as soon as possible. Giving us 3 harassment tools that play together quite nicely.

-  A big part of the philosophy in the early game is if they try to come attack us, cata's a tower + some worker hold position and a rag tag team of rax units can defend us from a disproportionate amount of supply while we focus on backstabbing with the raider gyro trusting that this will result favorably in a base trade scenario. the constant threat and fear of the backstab makes it very hard to move out for our opponent without first corralling our raiders back to our side of the map but this often is not easy or quick and is the premise of our early safety.

## Mid game

I honestly don't even know where to start because you can take it in so many directions and there could be many early game divergent points so I think I need to keep it simple and recommend a few things I like and remind you gyros should be giving you great scouting info react the best you can.

**I usually wind up doing 1 of 3 things depending on how the early game went.**
- Quick third: into a quick 4th off 1-1-1 ( typically I am only using 2 of the 3 buildings at a time to produce from depending on what I scout and what units I think will be most useful which actually makes this a very similar setup to the normal midgame skeleton albeit somewhat slower. or with the downside of later forges 
- Late third: ill try to just spend most of my focus being as abusive as I can with my harassment tools (raiders / gyro / cata drop) and really spamming upgrades on the 1-1-1 setup before eventually adding 2 more workshops and accepting staying focused on good trades from an eventual super army and being annoying with my harassment tools. look to take a 4th when main has ~1000 gold left on it. >After maxing literally try to double expand you have a very upgraded large army now is your time to seize the map!
- I believe this setup often leads into powerful 3 base all ins going full commitment with the workshops or throwing down a bunch of dens to flood wolves that can tank for cata archer core with either setup the gyros try to find a weak target or if nothing else protect from backstabs to our rallied push then coming in and hold positioning over the catas to help dps melee units without flying to their deaths to fast. ( bring a handful of workers for towers + auto repair)

# Late game

Transitions where you played 1-1-1 but chose to play rax as your primary composition and use cata and gyro as support? Build a church when you can fit it in, build it if you cant fit it in. Your rax units will love their new health care plan and when the priests aren't healing your units they can commit war crimes by making harassing catapults invisible, also invis grants +2 atk for its duration which is enough to make a 0-0 cata 2 shot workers. (this probably belongs in midgame or lategame)


You made it to the late game and have upgraded mech? You are probably having a good time. Ideas on how to have an even better time? Your gluttony disgusts me.

At some point before max out get a few mages for slow field. Engaging into slowfield + cata army is awful they have to bait out the slow field and come from multiple sides to try and get you to burn mana casting it in multiple spots then retreating. Slow field is a powerful lever for cata armies.
